Akshay Sethi
Email: asethi09[at]in.ibm.com

Akshay Sethi is a Research Engineer at IBM Research (IBM-IRL, New Delhi) working in the field of Artificial Intelligence, previously graduated from IIIT-Delhi working primarily with Dr. Mayank Vatsa and Dr. Richa Singh on deep learning, computer vision and biometrics for his undergraduate thesis.

He spent Summer 2017 at IBM Research (IBM-IRL, Bangalore) working with the Software Engineering for AI team on the DARVIZ Deep learning platform.
