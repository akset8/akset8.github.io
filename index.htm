Akshay Sethi is a Research Engineer at Borealis AI, Waterloo, Canada working in the field of Artificial Intelligence, specifically Knowledge Graphs and NLP. He was previously at IBM Research (IBM-IRL, Bangalore) and graduated from IIIT-Delhi working primarily with Dr. Mayank Vatsa and Dr. Richa Singh on deep learning, computer vision and biometrics for his undergraduate thesis.
